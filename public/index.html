Findings

💡 Key Insights

What the data says once all 10 countries are joined into one model.

🎵

Reach

Music & Entertainment dominate global trending views by a wide margin.

🌍

Country mix

India & Russia skew to Entertainment/Film; US & GB skew to Music & Comedy.

🤝

Engagement

Nonprofits & Education drive the highest engagement rate despite lower raw views.

⚡

Speed

Most videos trend fast — median ~1 day, average ~7.6 days to trend.

🏷️

Distribution

Most trending videos sit in the 100K–1M views band; 20M+ is rare.

Engineering

🔧 Cleaning Pipeline

All 10 countries joined into one analysis-ready fact table.

Joins 10 countries, repairs latin1 mojibake with ftfy (so Japanese / Korean / Cyrillic / accented titles render correctly), maps per-country categories, derives engagement metrics & days_to_trend, and drops nulls / duplicates / impossible rows.

Result: 375,942 → 342,656 rows, 0 nulls. See cleaning_script/cleaning_all_countries.py and BUILD_GUIDE.md.
